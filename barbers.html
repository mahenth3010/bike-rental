Meet Our Barbers

Jake

With over 10 years of experience in the barbering industry, Jake specializes in modern haircuts and beard grooming. His attention to detail and passion for styling make him a favorite among our clients. Whether you're looking for a classic cut or a trendy style, Jake has the skills to deliver.

Edwin

Edwin is a master barber with 8 years of experience. He excels in traditional barbering techniques and is known for his precision in fades and straight razor shaves. Edwin's friendly demeanor and dedication to his craft ensure that every client leaves the chair looking and feeling their best.
